Test parameters - Configuration

How to enter or edit a parameter?

Step 1: Just enter or edit the parameter values.

Step 2: Click the button..

What do the following items mean?
Msr unit: Unit of measurement e.g. ccm (cubic centimeter), ml (millimeter), ppm (parts per million), etc.

Median: Average normal value.

Upper boundary: The upper limit of normal values range.

Lower boundary: The lower limit of normal values range.

Upper critical: The upper value considered to begin as critical.

Lower critical: The lower value considered to begin as critical.

Upper toxic: The upper value considered to begin as toxic/deadly.

Lower toxic: The lower value considered to begin as toxic/deadly.

Note:
If you decide to cancel or if you are finished editing the values, click the button.
