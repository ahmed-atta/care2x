How to search an insurance company

Step 1

Enter either a full information or a few letters from the insurance company's information, like for example name, ID, or email address.

Example 1: enter "3321000012" or "3321".
Example 2: enter "AAA Security" or "AAA".
Example 3: enter "contact@aaa.com" or "contact@a".

Note: You can also use the % or * wildcard.

Step 2
Click the button to start the search.
Step 3
If the search finds a result or results, a list will be displayed.
To see the data of the insurance company you are looking for, click the its ID or name.
Note
If you want to send an email to the company, click on its email address and the email client will be launched.
Note
If you decide to cancel search click the button .
