Searching for an employee

How to find the employee?

Step 1: Enter either a full information or a few letters from a employee's information, like for example first name, or family name, or the encounter number.

Example 1: enter "21000012" or "12".
Example 2: enter "Guerero" or "gue".
Example 3: enter "Alfredo" or "Alf".

Step 2: Click the button to start searching.

Step 3: If the search finds a result, select the right employee from the displayed list by clicking its button.
