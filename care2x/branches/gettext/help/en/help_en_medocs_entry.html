Medocs - New document

How to create a new medocs document?

Step 1: Click "Enter new record" to generate a new form.

Step 2: Type the extra information if available.

Step 3: Select the appropriate radio button for medical advice received by the patient.

Step 4: Type the textual diagnoses.

Step 5: Type the textual therapies.

How to save the medocs document?
Step: Click the button.
How to list available medocs document?
Step: Click the "Show document list" below the document form.
How to view a medocs document?
Step: Click the document's icon.
