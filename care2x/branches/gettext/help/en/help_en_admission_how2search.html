How to search a patient

Step 1

Enter either a full information or a few letters from a patient's information, like for example patient's encounter number, or family name, or given name.

Example 1: enter "21000012" or "12".
Example 2: enter "Guerero" or "gue".
Example 3: enter "Alfredo" or "Alf".

You can also use the % and * wildcards.

Step 2
Click the button to start the search.
Step 3
If the search finds a result or results, a list will be displayed.
To see the admission data of the patient you are looking for, click the icon following it.
