Medocs - Finding the patient

How to create a new medocs document?

Step 1: If the patient is in the resulting list, click its icon to display its available medocs documents.

Step 3: Click "Enter new record" to generate a new form.

Step 4: Type the "Extra information" if available.

Step 5: Select the appropriate radio button for medical advice received by the patient.

Step 6: Type the textual diagnoses.

Step 7: Type the textual therapies.
