Create or edit doctors' list

How to add a doctor to the list?

Step 1: Find the doctor first using the displayed search function.

Step 2: If the doctor is in the resulting list, click its button.

The displayed department is wrong. I want to change to the right department.
Step 1: Select the department on the "Change department: " field.
Step 2: Click the button to change to the selected department.
I want to delete a doctor from the list
Step 1: Click the corresponding icon.

Step 2: Click "OK" if you are sure to delete, otherwise click "Cancel" to cancel deletion.
