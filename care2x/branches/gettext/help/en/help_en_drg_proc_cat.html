DRG - Procedure - Category

How to set the category of the procedure?

Step 1: Click the selection box corresponding to the procedure.

Step 2: Select and click the category on the list.

What are the categories?
Main
Supplemental
How to set the main procedure?
Step 1: Click the selection box corresponding to the procedure.

Step 2: Click the "Main" category on the list.

How to change the main procedure?
Step 1: Click the selection box corresponding to the procedure.

Step 2: Click the "Main" category on the list.
