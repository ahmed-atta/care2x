Calendar

Tip:

If you click on a date in the calendar, a window will pop up showing several options for both the logged-in user and department.
How to display the previous month?
Step 1: Click the link " << previous month " on the upper left part of the calendar.
How to display the following month?
Step 1: Click the link " next month >> " on the upper right part of the calendar.
How to select a month directly?
Step 1: Select the month from the selection box " Month ".
Step 2: Enter the year in the " Year " field.
