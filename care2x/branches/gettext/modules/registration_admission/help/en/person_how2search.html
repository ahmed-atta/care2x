How to search a person

Step 1

Enter either a full information or a few letters from a person's information, like for example first name, or family name, or the PID (person identifier).

Example 1: enter "21000012" or "12".
Example 2: enter "Guerero" or "gue".
Example 3: enter "Alfredo" or "Alf".

Note: You can also use the % or * wildcard.

Step 2
Click the button to start the search.
Step 3
If the search finds a result or results, a list will be displayed.
To see the admission data of the person you are looking for, click the button following it.
