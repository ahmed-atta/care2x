What can I do here?

This is a typical submenu. The submenu is generally arranged into three columns.

  • Column 1 : Icons. The icons are not clickable.
  • Column 2 : Menu items. These are the clickable items which will either activate a function or lead to further functions or submenus. If you want to activate or open the function, click on the item.
  • Column 3 : Descriptions. These are brief descriptions or overviews of the functions or purposes of the menu items in the column 2.
