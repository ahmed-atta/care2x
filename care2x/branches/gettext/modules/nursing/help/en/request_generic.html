Test request

How to create a test request?

Step 1: Check the "Visit requested" checkbox if physician's visit to the patient is requested.

Step 2: Check the "Patient can be ordered" checkbox if patient is mobile and can be ordered to the clinic or department.

Step 3: Enter the diagnostic test being requested, the known diagnoses, inquiries, and other supplemental information.

Step 4: Enter the requesting physician's name.
