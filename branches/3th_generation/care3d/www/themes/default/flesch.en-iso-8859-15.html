Flesch Score

The Flesch score is the result of some basic readability metrics performed on the article you've written. The number of words, the number of sentences, and the number of total syllables have been taken into account. The Flesch score for a sentence represents the readability of the text, and is a number usually between 0 and 100. A basic breakdown of scores is:

  • 90 to 100 5th grade
  • 80 to 90 6th grade
  • 70 to 80 7th grade
  • 60 to 70 8th and 9th grade
  • 50 to 60 10th to 12th grade (high school)
  • 30 to 50 college
  • 0 to 30 college graduate
